Ticket Submission

When you encounter a problem with:

  • the grid infrastructure
  • any of the applications running on the grid
  • or the usability of the grid

you are strongly encouraged to inform the grid operators. This can be done by filling in the form at https://rt.smscg.ch. The submission of the form will trigger the creation of a ticket, which will then be assigned to a person that can resolve the problem.

Thank you.
