VOMS Error

An error occured while trying to retrieve your VO credentials. The respective VOMS servers could not been contacted.
There are many possible reasons for this, among which also a temporary unavailability of the VOMS servers.
Please try therefore again at a later point in time before reporting the error to the administrator of this server.

Thank you very much.

Remedy

As a user you can't do anything but try again later. As a server administrator you should give a look to the logs.
